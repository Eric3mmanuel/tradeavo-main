About Us

TradeAvo Limited

TradeAvo Limited is a Kenyan-based agribusiness company specializing in the sourcing, packing, and export of premium-quality fresh avocados, alongside a selected range of fresh fruits and vegetables. Drawing from Kenya’s diverse agricultural regions, we work closely with certified local farmers to ensure consistent supply, full traceability, and strict compliance with international food safety and quality standards. Avocados remain at the heart of our operations, and we are committed to delivering produce that meets the expectations of global markets in the Middle East, Europe, and Asia. Our export processes emphasize freshness, careful handling, and sustainable farming practices to preserve quality from farm to destination. Beyond avocados, TradeAvo Limited also supplies carefully sourced fruits and vegetables, applying the same standards of ethical sourcing, quality control, and reliability. We focus on building long-term partnerships with growers, packhouses, and international buyers, ensuring transparency, consistency, and shared growth across the supply chain.

Our Services

Services That We Offer Our Esteemed Customers

Export packaging and labeling

We provide export-standard packaging and labeling tailored to international market requirements. Our packaging ensures produce freshness, protection during transit, and full compliance with buyer, airline, and shipping regulations, while our labeling guarantees traceability and brand consistency.

Quality control and grading

Every batch of produce undergoes strict quality control and grading processes. We carefully inspect for size, ripeness, appearance, and overall condition to ensure only premium-grade fruits and vegetables meet export standards and buyer expectations.

Phytosanitary and export documentation

We handle all required phytosanitary certifications and export documentation to ensure smooth customs clearance. Our team ensures full compliance with destination country regulations, reducing delays and ensuring seamless cross-border trade.

Sea freight and air freight coordination

We coordinate reliable sea and air freight solutions based on product type, shelf life, and delivery timelines. Through trusted logistics partners, we ensure efficient handling, temperature control, and timely delivery to international markets.

Buyer-specific packing specifications

We customize packing formats to meet individual buyer requirements, including carton sizes, weights, branding, and labeling preferences. This flexibility ensures consistency, compliance, and satisfaction across different markets and clients.
